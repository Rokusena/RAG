Customer Loyalty and Referral Program
=======================================

1. Program Overview
At AutoGroup Motors, we value long-term relationships with our customers. Our Loyalty and Referral Program is designed to reward returning customers and those who recommend us to friends, family, and colleagues. The program is free to join and offers tangible benefits on future purchases, service, and accessories.

2. Loyalty Tiers
Upon completing your first purchase at AutoGroup Motors, you are automatically enrolled in our loyalty program at the Bronze tier. As you continue to engage with us through purchases and services, you progress through the tiers:

Bronze (1st purchase):
- 10% discount on labor for all service work at our facility
- Priority scheduling for service appointments
- Complimentary multi-point inspection every 6 months for the first year
- Access to exclusive pre-sale notifications for new inventory arrivals

Silver (2nd purchase or EUR 3,000+ in cumulative service spending):
- All Bronze benefits
- 15% discount on labor
- One free Small Service package per year (valued at EUR 99)
- Complimentary courtesy vehicle during service visits lasting over 4 hours
- Invitation to annual customer appreciation events

Gold (3rd purchase or EUR 7,000+ in cumulative service spending):
- All Silver benefits
- 20% discount on labor and 10% discount on parts
- One free Full Service package per year (valued at EUR 199)
- Priority access to limited-edition or high-demand vehicles
- Dedicated account manager for all sales and service inquiries
- Complimentary annual vehicle detailing package (valued at EUR 99)

3. Referral Rewards
When you refer a new customer to AutoGroup Motors and they complete a vehicle purchase, both you and the referred customer receive a reward:

- Referring customer: EUR 200 service credit or EUR 150 cash payment (your choice)
- Referred customer: EUR 100 service credit applied to their new account

There is no limit to the number of referrals you can make. Referrals are tracked through our CRM system. To ensure your referral is recorded, the referred customer should mention your name at the time of their first visit or include it in their online inquiry form. Referral rewards are processed within 14 business days of the referred customer's purchase completion.

4. Service Credits
Service credits earned through the loyalty program or referral rewards can be applied to any service, maintenance, parts purchase, or accessory purchase at our dealership. Credits do not expire as long as the customer's loyalty account remains active (at least one transaction per 24 months). Credits are non-transferable and cannot be redeemed for cash unless explicitly stated in the referral reward terms.

5. Special Promotions
Loyalty program members receive early access to seasonal promotions, such as discounted winter tire packages, spring maintenance specials, and holiday sales events. Members are notified via email and SMS at least 48 hours before promotions are made available to the general public. To manage your communication preferences, log into your account at www.autogroupmotors.lt/loyalty or contact our customer service team.

6. Fleet and Corporate Loyalty
Business customers with three or more vehicles serviced or purchased through AutoGroup Motors qualify for our Corporate Loyalty tier, which offers additional benefits including dedicated fleet management support, volume pricing on parts and service, consolidated billing, and quarterly fleet health reports.

7. How to Check Your Status
You can check your current loyalty tier, referral history, and available credits at any time by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team at loyalty@autogroupmotors.lt. Account statements are also sent via email quarterly.

8. Terms and Conditions
AutoGroup Motors reserves the right to modify the loyalty and referral program terms at any time, with 30 days' advance notice provided to active members. Benefits accrued before any program changes will be honored in full. The program is governed by Lithuanian law and any disputes will be resolved in accordance with the Lithuanian Consumer Rights Protection Law.
