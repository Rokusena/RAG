3. Vehicle Condition Grades
Each vehicle in our stock is assigned a condition grade:

- Grade A (Excellent): Near-new condition, no visible wear, under 20,000 km
- Grade B (Very Good): Minor cosmetic wear, well-maintained, under 50,000 km
- Grade C (Good): Normal wear consistent with age and mileage, fully serviced
- Grade D (Fair): May require minor cosmetic attention, priced accordingly
