Employee Health and Benefits Package
=====================================
CONFIDENTIAL — FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY

1. Overview
AutoGroup Motors provides a comprehensive benefits package to all full-time employees who have completed their probation period. Part-time employees working 20 or more hours per week are eligible for prorated benefits. This document outlines health insurance, wellness programs, leave policies, and additional perks.

2. Health Insurance

a. Private Health Insurance
AutoGroup Motors partners with Compensa Vienna Insurance Group to provide supplementary private health insurance for all eligible employees.

Coverage includes:

- Outpatient care: GP and specialist consultations, diagnostics, laboratory tests — up to EUR 2,000 per year
- Dental care: Preventive check-ups, fillings, extractions — up to EUR 500 per year
- Inpatient care: Hospital stays, surgeries, rehabilitation — up to EUR 15,000 per year
- Prescription medications: 80% reimbursement on prescribed medicines, up to EUR 400 per year
- Mental health: Up to 12 psychologist or psychotherapist sessions per year, fully covered
- Optical care: Eye exams and EUR 150 allowance toward corrective lenses or contact lenses per year

The employer covers 100% of the insurance premium for the employee. Family members (spouse and children under 18) may be added at a discounted group rate of EUR 45 per person per month, payable via salary deduction.

b. Mandatory State Health Insurance (PSD)
In addition to private insurance, all employees are covered under Lithuania's mandatory state health insurance (Privalomasis Sveikatos Draudimas). Employer contributions are made automatically as part of payroll taxes.

3. Wellness Program

AutoGroup Motors encourages a healthy lifestyle through the following initiatives:

- Gym membership subsidy: EUR 30 per month reimbursement toward any gym or fitness studio membership
- Annual health check-up: Fully paid comprehensive health screening at a partner clinic (Northway Medical Centre), scheduled once per year
- Ergonomic workstation assessment: Available upon request for office-based employees
- Flu vaccination: Free annual flu vaccination offered on-site each autumn
- Fruit and healthy snacks: Provided daily in the staff break room

4. Leave Entitlements

a. Annual Leave
- Standard: 20 working days per calendar year (full-time employees)
- After 5 years of service: 25 working days per calendar year
- After 10 years of service: 28 working days per calendar year

Unused annual leave may be carried over to the following year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Remaining unused days are forfeited.

b. Sick Leave
- First 2 days of illness: Paid at 80% of salary by the employer
- Day 3 onward: Covered by SODRA (State Social Insurance Fund) at 62.06% of compensatory salary
- Employees must provide a medical certificate for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days

c. Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 126 calendar days (70 days before and 56 days after birth), paid by SODRA
- Paternity leave: 30 calendar days, paid by SODRA
- Childcare leave: Up to 2 years, paid by SODRA at decreasing rates
- AutoGroup Motors guarantees the employee's position or an equivalent role upon return

d. Additional Leave
- Bereavement leave: 3 paid days for the death of an immediate family member
- Marriage leave: 3 paid days
- Study leave: Up to 5 unpaid days per year for job-related education (with manager approval)
- Volunteering leave: 1 paid day per year for approved community service activities

5. Retirement and Savings

- Pension contributions: Standard SODRA contributions (employer and employee shares) are applied in accordance with Lithuanian law
- Voluntary pension (III pillar): AutoGroup Motors matches employee voluntary pension contributions up to EUR 50 per month
- The company partners with SEB bank for voluntary pension plan administration

6. Additional Benefits

- Company vehicle: Available for Sales Managers and above, subject to the company car policy
- Fuel allowance: EUR 100 per month for employees who use personal vehicles for business purposes (requires approval)
- Mobile phone: Company phone and plan provided for all sales, management, and logistics roles
- Employee vehicle purchase discount: 3% discount off listed price on any vehicle in stock, available once per calendar year
- Professional development: Up to EUR 500 per year for job-related courses, certifications, or conferences (with HR approval)
- Team events: Quarterly team-building events and an annual company celebration funded by the company
- Free parking: Designated employee parking spaces at the dealership

7. Enrolment and Changes

New employees are automatically enrolled in the health insurance plan upon completion of probation. To add family members, change beneficiaries, or opt into the voluntary pension scheme, submit a request to hr@autogroupmotors.lt. Changes are processed on the 1st of the following month.

8. Contact

For benefits-related questions, contact hr@autogroupmotors.lt or speak directly with the HR Specialist during office hours (Monday to Friday, 9:00–18:00).
