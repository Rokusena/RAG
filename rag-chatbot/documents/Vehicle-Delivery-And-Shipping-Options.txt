Vehicle Delivery and Shipping Options
=======================================

1. Overview
AutoGroup Motors offers multiple delivery options to ensure our customers receive their purchased vehicle in the most convenient way possible. Whether you are located in Vilnius, elsewhere in Lithuania, or in a neighboring Baltic state, we have a delivery solution for you.

2. Dealership Pickup
The simplest and most common option is picking up your vehicle at our dealership located at Gedimino pr. 45, Vilnius. Upon completion of all paperwork, payment, and registration, we prepare the vehicle for handover. This preparation includes a full exterior wash, interior cleaning, fluid top-off, and tire pressure check. A sales consultant will walk you through the vehicle's features, pair your phone via Bluetooth, and ensure you are comfortable with all controls before you drive away.

Dealership pickup is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 to 18:00, and Saturday, 9:00 to 15:00. We recommend scheduling your pickup at least one day in advance so we can have the vehicle fully prepared. There is no additional charge for dealership pickup.

3. Local Delivery (Vilnius and Surrounding Area)
For customers within Vilnius city limits and the surrounding area (up to 30 km from our dealership), we offer complimentary local delivery. A member of our team will deliver the vehicle to your home or office at a time convenient for you. All handover documentation is completed on-site, and our delivery driver will provide a brief vehicle orientation.

Local deliveries are available Monday through Saturday. Please allow at least 24 hours' notice when scheduling.

4. National Delivery (Lithuania)
For customers located outside the Vilnius area but within Lithuania, we offer a vehicle transport service via enclosed or open car carrier. Delivery times and fees depend on the destination:

- Kaunas: EUR 79, delivery within 1 to 2 business days
- Klaipėda: EUR 129, delivery within 2 to 3 business days
- Šiauliai: EUR 119, delivery within 2 to 3 business days
- Panevėžys: EUR 99, delivery within 1 to 2 business days
- Other locations: Pricing available upon request, generally EUR 69 to EUR 159

All national deliveries include basic transport insurance covering the vehicle's full declared value during transit. The delivery fee is waived for purchases over EUR 20,000.

5. International Delivery (Baltic States)
We deliver vehicles to Latvia and Estonia through our logistics partners. International delivery fees are as follows:

- Riga, Latvia: EUR 199, delivery within 3 to 5 business days
- Tallinn, Estonia: EUR 299, delivery within 5 to 7 business days
- Other Baltic locations: Pricing available upon request

International deliveries require the customer to handle vehicle registration in their home country. AutoGroup Motors provides all necessary export documentation, including the original vehicle title, purchase invoice, and any customs-related paperwork. We can also recommend registration agents in Latvia and Estonia if needed.

6. Self-Drive Delivery
Customers who prefer to drive their new vehicle home themselves but are located far away can take advantage of our self-drive delivery support. We provide temporary transit plates valid for up to 30 days, allowing you to legally drive the vehicle to your home location for registration. The cost for temporary transit plates is EUR 35, and the plates must be returned to Regitra or our dealership once permanent registration is completed.

7. Delivery Condition Guarantee
Regardless of the delivery method chosen, AutoGroup Motors guarantees that the vehicle will arrive in the same condition as documented at the time of sale. Photographs of the vehicle are taken before dispatch and shared with the customer via email. Upon receipt, the customer is asked to inspect the vehicle and sign a delivery acceptance form. Any discrepancies must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
