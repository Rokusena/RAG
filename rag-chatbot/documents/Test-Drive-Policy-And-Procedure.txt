Test Drive Policy and Procedure
=================================

1. Purpose
AutoGroup Motors encourages all prospective buyers to test drive vehicles before making a purchase decision. A test drive allows you to experience the vehicle's handling, comfort, performance, and features firsthand. This document outlines the requirements and procedures for scheduling and conducting a test drive.

2. Eligibility Requirements
To be eligible for a test drive, the customer must:
- Be at least 18 years old
- Hold a valid driving license appropriate for the vehicle category (Category B for standard passenger vehicles)
- Present a valid government-issued identification document (Lithuanian ID card, passport, or EU residence permit)
- Not appear to be under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any substances that impair driving ability

Customers under the age of 24 or those with a driving license issued less than 2 years ago may be required to be accompanied by a dealership representative during the test drive. This policy exists for insurance purposes and the safety of all parties involved.

3. Scheduling a Test Drive
Test drives can be scheduled in the following ways:
- Online: Fill out the test drive request form at www.autogroupmotors.lt/test-drive, selecting your preferred vehicle, date, and time slot.
- Phone: Call +370 5 123 4567 during business hours.
- In person: Walk-in test drives are available subject to vehicle and staff availability. We recommend scheduling in advance to guarantee your preferred vehicle is prepared and available.

Test drive appointments are available Monday through Friday, 9:00 to 17:00, and Saturday, 9:00 to 14:00. Each test drive slot is 30 minutes, which includes paperwork, a vehicle walkthrough with the sales consultant, and approximately 15 to 20 minutes of actual driving time.

4. Test Drive Procedure
Before the drive:
- The customer signs a test drive agreement form, which acknowledges responsibility for any traffic violations committed during the drive and outlines the insurance coverage and excess amounts.
- A sales consultant provides a brief overview of the vehicle's key features, controls, and any specific characteristics the driver should be aware of.
- The customer and sales consultant conduct a walkaround of the vehicle to note any pre-existing cosmetic damage on the condition report.

During the drive:
- The sales consultant accompanies the customer on a predetermined route designed to include city driving, highway sections, and parking maneuvers. The route covers approximately 10 to 15 kilometers.
- Customers must obey all traffic laws and speed limits at all times. Aggressive driving, excessive speeding, or reckless behavior will result in the immediate termination of the test drive.
- Customers may request adjustments to the route to test specific road conditions relevant to their daily commute (for example, cobblestone streets or highway on-ramps), subject to the consultant's approval.

After the drive:
- The vehicle is inspected upon return for any new damage.
- The customer is invited to discuss their experience, ask questions, and explore financing or trade-in options if interested in proceeding with a purchase.

5. Insurance and Liability
All test drive vehicles are covered by AutoGroup Motors' commercial insurance policy. However, in the event of an accident caused by the test driver's negligence or violation of traffic laws, the customer is responsible for the insurance excess amount of up to EUR 500. The customer is also fully responsible for any traffic fines incurred during the test drive.

6. Vehicles Available for Test Drives
Most vehicles in our inventory are available for test drives. However, recently arrived vehicles that have not yet completed our inspection process, vehicles with pending service work, or vehicles that are reserved or sold may not be available. High-value or specialty vehicles may require a refundable deposit of EUR 100 before the test drive, which is returned upon safe completion of the drive.

7. Remote or Home Test Drives
For customers located outside Vilnius or with mobility limitations, we offer a remote test drive service. A member of our team will deliver the vehicle to a mutually agreed-upon location within a 30-kilometer radius of our dealership for the test drive. This service is available by appointment only and subject to a logistics fee of EUR 25. The fee is waived if the customer proceeds with a purchase.

8. Contact
For test drive bookings, contact sales@autogroupmotors.lt or call +370 5 123 4567.
