COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------
HR CARs is a licensed used car dealership registered in the Republic of Lithuania. Our business
registration number is 305XXXXXX, and we operate under Lithuanian Law on Road Transport and
relevant EU consumer protection directives. Our primary dealership is located in Vilnius, Lithuania.
We are a small, customer-first team of up to 10 professionals committed to honest, transparent
vehicle sales and after-sales support. This document outlines the internal rules and standards
that all employees must follow in their day-to-day operations.

1. WORKING HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
---------------------------------
1.1 Official dealership operating hours are Monday through Saturday, 09:00 to 18:00 (local time).
    The dealership is closed on Sundays and Lithuanian public holidays unless management
    announces otherwise in advance.

1.2 All employees must arrive at the dealership premises no later than 08:45 — 15 minutes before
    opening — to prepare the showroom, review new inventory updates, check emails, and ensure
    the premises are clean and presentable for customers.

1.3 Lunch breaks are 30 minutes and must be staggered among staff so that at least one salesperson
    and one administrative/support staff member are present on the floor at all times during
    business hours.

1.4 Overtime must be pre-approved by management. Employees who work overtime on weekdays will be
    compensated at 1.5x their hourly rate per Lithuanian Labour Code requirements. Saturday work
    within normal shift hours is considered standard.

1.5 Punctuality is essential. Three unexcused late arrivals within a single calendar month will
    result in a formal written warning. Repeated violations may lead to disciplinary action up
    to and including termination, following the procedures set out in the Lithuanian Labour Code.

1.6 Employees who are unable to attend work must notify their direct manager no later than 08:00
    on the day of absence. Medical certificates (nedarbingumo lapeliai) must be submitted within
    3 working days of returning to work.

2. DRESS CODE AND PERSONAL PRESENTATION
-----------------------------------------
2.1 All sales staff must wear the company-issued HR CARs polo shirt or jacket. Trousers must be
    clean, dark-coloured, and professional. Trainers or casual footwear are not permitted on the
    sales floor.

2.2 Mechanic and inspection staff must wear the company-issued overalls and appropriate personal
    protective equipment (PPE) at all times when working in the vehicle inspection area.

2.3 Name badges must be worn visibly at all times during working hours.

2.4 Personal hygiene standards must be maintained at a professional level at all times.

3. CUSTOMER INTERACTION STANDARDS
------------------------------------
3.1 Every customer who enters the dealership must be greeted verbally within 2 minutes of arrival.
    The greeting must be friendly, professional, and in the customer's preferred language
    (Lithuanian, Russian, or English as applicable). Standard greeting: "Sveiki atvykę į HR CARs!
    Kuo galiu jums padėti?" or the equivalent in Russian/English.

3.2 Employees must never make a customer feel pressured or uncomfortable. High-pressure sales
    tactics are strictly prohibited.

3.3 All information provided to customers about vehicles — including mileage, technical condition,
    accident history, and pricing — must be accurate and verifiable. Deliberately misleading a
    customer is grounds for immediate dismissal and may expose the company to legal liability.

3.4 Phone inquiries must be answered within 3 rings during business hours. If a staff member
    cannot answer, calls must be returned within 2 hours. All missed calls must be logged in
    the CRM system.

3.5 Online inquiries (from autoplius.lt, autogidas.lt, or company email) must receive an initial
    response within 1 business hour during working hours.

3.6 Complaints must be taken seriously and escalated to the manager immediately if the employee
    cannot resolve the issue independently. All complaints must be logged in the customer
    service register.

3.7 Staff must not discuss competitor dealerships negatively. Focus on HR CARs' value proposition:
    inspected vehicles, warranty coverage, transparent pricing, and professional after-sales service.

4. VEHICLE INSPECTION PROCEDURES
-----------------------------------
4.1 Every vehicle acquired for inventory must undergo a full pre-sale inspection within 48 hours
    of arriving on the lot.

4.2 The inspection must be performed by a qualified mechanic and must include the following:
    a) Visual exterior inspection — body condition, paintwork, glass, lights, tyres, alloys
    b) Visual interior inspection — upholstery, dashboard, electronics, air conditioning
    c) Under-bonnet inspection — engine, fluids, belts, battery
    d) Undercarriage inspection — chassis, suspension, exhaust, brake lines
    e) Road test — minimum 10 km, testing acceleration, braking, steering, gearbox, clutch
    f) OBD-II diagnostics scan — all fault codes must be logged and addressed
    g) Vehicle history check via CarVertical or equivalent — to verify mileage, accidents,
       ownership history, and outstanding finance
    h) Document verification — registration certificate (техпаспорт / techninis pasas),
       roadworthiness certificate (technine apžiūra), and any available service records

4.3 All inspection findings must be recorded in the Vehicle Inspection Report (VIR) form and
    stored digitally in the company's management system. A printed copy must be placed in the
    vehicle's folder.

4.4 Any defects found during inspection must be categorised:
    - Category A (Minor): cosmetic issues that do not affect safety or driveability (e.g., small
      scratches, worn interior trim). These may be listed on the vehicle's condition sheet and
      reflected in the pricing.
    - Category B (Significant): mechanical or safety-relevant issues that must be repaired before
      the vehicle can be listed for sale (e.g., brake pad wear below 3mm, tyre tread below legal
      limit, fault codes relating to safety systems).
    - Category C (Major): structural or very costly issues that require management approval before
      proceeding. Management will decide whether to repair, auction, or reject the vehicle.

4.5 No vehicle may be listed for sale or shown to customers until the inspection is complete and
    all Category B defects have been resolved.

4.6 Vehicles must also have a valid Lithuanian roadworthiness test (techninė apžiūra) or one
    must be arranged before sale if the existing one has less than 3 months remaining.

5. PRICING AND NEGOTIATION POLICY
------------------------------------
5.1 All listing prices are set by management and entered into the CRM/inventory system. Prices
    on autoplius.lt and other platforms must match the internal listing price at all times.

5.2 Sales staff may offer a discount of up to 3% on the listed price without manager approval.
    Discounts above 3% require written approval from the manager.

5.3 Trade-in valuations must be conducted using current market data (autoplius.lt average,
    eurotax, or carvertical estimate) and approved by the manager before being communicated
    to the customer.

6. RETURN AND WARRANTY POLICY
--------------------------------
6.1 HR CARs offers a 14-day return policy on all vehicles sold, in accordance with Lithuanian
    consumer protection law. The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as sold, with
    no additional mileage exceeding 500 km beyond the odometer reading at point of sale.

6.2 All vehicles sold by HR CARs are covered by a 3-month mechanical warranty from the date
    of sale. The warranty covers:
    - Engine and gearbox mechanical failures not attributable to misuse or accident
    - Electrical system faults not caused by external damage
    - Cooling system failures

6.3 The warranty does NOT cover:
    - Consumable parts (brake pads, tyres, wiper blades, bulbs, filters)
    - Damage resulting from accidents, misuse, or modification by the customer
    - Faults disclosed and documented on the Vehicle Condition Sheet at time of sale

6.4 Warranty claims must be submitted in writing (email or in person) within the 3-month period.
    HR CARs will assess the claim within 5 business days and arrange repair at our partner
    service centre if the claim is valid.

6.5 Extended warranty options beyond 3 months may be offered via third-party providers and
    presented to customers at the time of sale as optional add-ons.

7. DATA PROTECTION AND CONFIDENTIALITY
-----------------------------------------
7.1 All customer personal data collected during the sales process is handled in accordance with
    the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Lithuanian data protection laws.

7.2 Employees must not share customer data with third parties without explicit consent.

7.3 Internal pricing, supplier information, and margin data are strictly confidential and must
    not be disclosed outside the company.

8. HEALTH, SAFETY AND FACILITY RULES
---------------------------------------
8.1 The vehicle lot and showroom must be kept clean and tidy at all times. All employees share
    responsibility for maintaining the premises.

8.2 No smoking is permitted inside the showroom or within 5 metres of the entrance.

8.3 Test drive vehicles must always have a company employee present in the vehicle.

8.4 Vehicle keys must be stored in the designated key cabinet at all times when not in use.
    Keys must never be left in unattended vehicles on the lot.

8.5 Fire extinguishers are located at the showroom entrance and in the inspection bay. All
    employees must know their locations. Emergency exits must remain unobstructed at all times.

9. POLICY REVIEW
------------------
This policy document is reviewed annually by management. Employees will be notified of any
changes in writing. By continuing employment at HR CARs, all staff acknowledge and agree to
comply with the current version of this policy.

For questions regarding this policy, contact the HR CARs management team directly.
