2. Major Service Packages

Small Service (recommended every 15,000 km or 12 months):
Includes oil and filter change, tire inspection, brake inspection, fluid level check, battery test, and multi-point visual inspection.
Price: EUR 99 (petrol) / EUR 119 (diesel)

Full Service (recommended every 30,000 km or 24 months):
Includes everything in Small Service plus air filter replacement, cabin filter replacement, spark plug inspection or replacement, coolant level and condition check, transmission fluid check, drive belt inspection, and comprehensive diagnostic scan.
Price: EUR 199 (petrol) / EUR 239 (diesel)

Major Service (recommended every 60,000 km or 48 months):
Includes everything in Full Service plus timing belt or chain inspection, coolant system flush and replacement, transmission fluid replacement, power steering fluid replacement, and suspension component inspection.
Price: EUR 349 to EUR 599 (varies significantly by vehicle model and engine type)
