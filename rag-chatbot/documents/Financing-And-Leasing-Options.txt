Financing and Leasing Options
===============================

1. Introduction
AutoGroup Motors partners with leading Lithuanian and European financial institutions to offer flexible vehicle financing and leasing solutions. Whether you are an individual buyer, a self-employed professional, or a business looking to expand your fleet, we have options tailored to your needs and budget.

2. Consumer Auto Loans
We work with SEB Bank, Swedbank, Luminor, and Šiaulių Bankas to provide competitive auto loan rates. Current interest rates for pre-owned vehicles start from 4.9% APR for borrowers with strong credit history. Loan terms range from 12 to 84 months, with down payments as low as 10% of the vehicle purchase price.

To apply for financing, customers need to provide:
- A valid Lithuanian ID or passport
- Proof of income (last 3 months of payslips or tax declarations for self-employed individuals)
- Bank account statements for the last 3 months
- A completed loan application form (available at our dealership or online)

Pre-approval decisions are typically delivered within 2 business hours during working days. Final approval and contract signing can be completed the same day in most cases.

3. Business Leasing
For businesses registered in Lithuania, we offer operational and financial leasing through our partners. Operational leasing is ideal for companies that want to use a vehicle without owning it, with fixed monthly payments that include insurance, maintenance, and registration costs. Financial leasing suits businesses that want to own the vehicle at the end of the lease term.

Typical business leasing terms:
- Duration: 24 to 60 months
- Down payment: 10% to 30%
- Residual value: 0% to 30% (financial leasing) or pre-set by the leasing company (operational leasing)
- Monthly payments include VAT, which is deductible for VAT-registered companies

4. First-Time Buyer Program
We understand that first-time buyers may have limited credit history. Our First-Time Buyer Program offers special financing terms for individuals aged 18 to 25 who are purchasing their first vehicle. With a co-signer or a minimum 20% down payment, we can secure financing at rates starting from 6.5% APR. This program is available for vehicles priced up to EUR 15,000.

5. Trade-In Financing
Customers trading in their current vehicle can apply the trade-in value directly to the down payment on their new purchase. Our appraisal team provides fair market valuations based on current market conditions, vehicle condition, mileage, and service history. Trade-in appraisals are free and completed within 30 minutes at our dealership.

6. Insurance Bundling
We offer the option to bundle comprehensive vehicle insurance (KASKO) with your financing package. Partnering with ERGO, Gjensidige, and BTA, we can include insurance premiums in your monthly payment for a seamless ownership experience. Bundled insurance customers receive a 5% discount on the first year's premium.

7. Early Repayment and Refinancing
All our financing agreements allow early repayment without penalty fees. Customers who wish to refinance their existing auto loan through one of our partner banks can do so at any time. Our finance team will assist with paperwork and ensure a smooth transition.
