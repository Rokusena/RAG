Returns, Refunds, and Dispute Resolution Policy
==================================================

1. Overview
AutoGroup Motors is committed to customer satisfaction. While we thoroughly inspect and accurately represent every vehicle we sell, we understand that issues may occasionally arise. This policy outlines your rights regarding returns, refunds, and how we handle disputes.

2. Cooling-Off Period
In accordance with Lithuanian consumer protection law, customers who purchase a vehicle through distance selling (online, phone, or other remote communication methods without physically visiting the dealership) are entitled to a 14-calendar-day cooling-off period from the date of delivery. During this period, you may return the vehicle for a full refund, provided that:
- The vehicle has not been driven more than 300 km beyond the odometer reading at delivery
- The vehicle is returned in the same condition as delivered, with no new damage, modifications, or alterations
- All original documentation, keys, accessories, and equipment are returned

Vehicles purchased in person at the dealership are not subject to the 14-day cooling-off period under Lithuanian law. However, AutoGroup Motors offers a voluntary 3-day satisfaction guarantee for in-person purchases, subject to the same conditions listed above. The 3-day guarantee begins from the date of delivery or pickup.

3. Refund Process
Approved refunds are processed within 14 business days of the returned vehicle passing our inspection. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. If the purchase was financed, AutoGroup Motors will coordinate with the financing institution to settle the loan and refund any remaining balance to the customer. Any trade-in vehicle applied to the original purchase will be returned to the customer if still in our possession, or its trade-in value will be refunded in cash if the vehicle has already been sold.

4. Defective Vehicle Claims
If a defect is discovered within the first 6 months of purchase that existed at the time of sale (as defined by Lithuanian Civil Code Article 6.363), the burden of proof lies with AutoGroup Motors to demonstrate that the defect was not present at the time of delivery. Customers who believe they have received a vehicle with a pre-existing defect should contact us immediately and provide a detailed description of the issue along with any supporting evidence (photographs, mechanic reports, diagnostic data).

Upon receiving a defect claim, we will:
a) Schedule an inspection at our service center within 5 business days
b) If the defect is confirmed and was pre-existing, offer the customer one of the following resolutions:
   - Free repair of the defect at our service center
   - A replacement vehicle of equivalent value and specification (subject to availability)
   - A partial refund proportional to the diminished value caused by the defect
   - A full refund and return of the vehicle (for significant defects that substantially affect the vehicle's use, safety, or value)

5. Dispute Resolution
We encourage customers to resolve any concerns directly with our customer service team first. Most issues can be addressed quickly through open communication. If a satisfactory resolution cannot be reached, the following options are available:

Internal Escalation: The customer may request that their complaint be escalated to the dealership manager, who will review the case and respond within 5 business days.

Lithuanian Consumer Rights Protection Authority (Valstybinė vartotojų teisių apsaugos tarnyba): Customers may file a formal complaint with this authority, which mediates disputes between consumers and businesses in Lithuania. More information is available at www.vvtat.lt.

European Online Dispute Resolution (ODR): For purchases made online, customers may submit a complaint through the EU ODR platform at https://ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr.

Court Proceedings: As a last resort, disputes may be resolved through the Lithuanian court system. AutoGroup Motors agrees to submit to the jurisdiction of Vilnius City District Court for all disputes arising from vehicle sales.

6. Limitation of Liability
AutoGroup Motors' total liability for any claim related to a vehicle purchase shall not exceed the original purchase price of the vehicle. This limitation does not affect the customer's statutory rights under Lithuanian consumer protection law or EU regulations.
