Employee Compensation and Pay Structure
========================================
CONFIDENTIAL — FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY

1. Overview
AutoGroup Motors is committed to offering competitive, transparent, and fair compensation to all employees. This document outlines the salary structure, bonus schemes, overtime policy, and payroll schedule applicable to all full-time and part-time staff.

2. Salary Bands by Role

a. Management

- General Manager: EUR 4,500–6,000 gross per month
- Sales Manager: EUR 3,200–4,200 gross per month
- Service Centre Manager: EUR 3,000–4,000 gross per month
- Finance and Administration Manager: EUR 3,000–3,800 gross per month

b. Sales Department

- Senior Sales Consultant: EUR 2,200–2,800 gross per month + commission
- Sales Consultant: EUR 1,600–2,200 gross per month + commission
- Junior Sales Consultant: EUR 1,200–1,600 gross per month + commission
- Sales Administrator: EUR 1,200–1,500 gross per month

c. Service and Technical

- Lead Mechanic / Workshop Foreman: EUR 2,400–3,000 gross per month
- Senior Mechanic: EUR 1,800–2,400 gross per month
- Mechanic: EUR 1,400–1,800 gross per month
- Junior Mechanic / Apprentice: EUR 900–1,200 gross per month
- Service Advisor: EUR 1,400–1,800 gross per month
- Parts Specialist: EUR 1,200–1,600 gross per month

d. Administration and Support

- Accountant: EUR 1,800–2,400 gross per month
- HR Specialist: EUR 1,600–2,200 gross per month
- Marketing Specialist: EUR 1,500–2,000 gross per month
- IT Support Specialist: EUR 1,600–2,200 gross per month
- Receptionist: EUR 1,000–1,300 gross per month
- Office Assistant: EUR 900–1,200 gross per month

e. Logistics

- Logistics Coordinator: EUR 1,500–2,000 gross per month
- Vehicle Delivery Driver: EUR 1,200–1,600 gross per month
- Warehouse Assistant: EUR 1,000–1,300 gross per month

3. Sales Commission Structure

Sales consultants earn commission on each vehicle sold, in addition to their base salary:

- Vehicles sold at or above target margin: 1.5% of vehicle gross profit
- Vehicles sold below target margin (approved discounts): 0.75% of vehicle gross profit
- Accessories and add-on products (extended warranties, insurance referrals): 5% of add-on revenue
- Monthly volume bonus: additional EUR 200 for every 5 vehicles sold beyond the monthly target of 8 vehicles

Commission is calculated monthly and paid with the following month's salary.

4. Annual Bonus

All employees are eligible for an annual performance bonus, subject to company performance and individual appraisal:

- Exceeds expectations: Up to 1.5 months' salary
- Meets expectations: Up to 1.0 month's salary
- Below expectations: No annual bonus

Annual bonuses are paid in January for the preceding calendar year.

5. Overtime Policy

Overtime must be approved in advance by the employee's direct manager.

- Weekday overtime (after 8 hours): 1.5x hourly rate
- Saturday overtime: 1.5x hourly rate
- Sunday and public holiday overtime: 2.0x hourly rate

Maximum overtime is 8 hours per week and 180 hours per calendar year, in accordance with Lithuanian labour law.

6. Payroll Schedule

- Pay period: Monthly (1st to last day of the month)
- Salary payment date: 10th of the following month
- Commission and bonus payment date: 10th of the month following calculation
- Payment method: Bank transfer to employee's designated Lithuanian bank account

7. Salary Reviews

Salary reviews are conducted annually in March. Adjustments are based on:

- Individual performance appraisal
- Market salary benchmarking
- Company financial performance
- Cost of living adjustments

Employees may request an off-cycle salary review after a significant change in responsibilities. Such requests should be submitted to the HR department.

8. Probation Period Pay

New employees on probation (standard 3 months) receive 90% of the lower end of their salary band. Upon successful completion of probation, salary is adjusted to the agreed rate within the band.

9. Contact

For compensation-related questions, contact hr@autogroupmotors.lt or speak directly with the HR Specialist.
