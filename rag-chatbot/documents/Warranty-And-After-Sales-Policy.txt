Warranty and After-Sales Service Policy
========================================

1. Overview
At AutoGroup Motors, we stand behind every vehicle we sell. Our warranty and after-sales service policy is designed to give our customers peace of mind and ensure long-term satisfaction with their purchase. This document outlines the warranty coverage, service procedures, and customer support channels available to all buyers.

2. Standard Warranty Coverage
Every pre-owned vehicle sold by AutoGroup Motors comes with a minimum 3-month or 5,000-kilometer powertrain warranty, whichever comes first. This warranty covers the engine, transmission, differential, drive axles, and transfer case. Repairs under warranty are performed at our authorized service center located at Gedimino pr. 45, Vilnius, or at any partner workshop listed in the Approved Service Network document.

New vehicles carry the full manufacturer warranty, which typically ranges from 2 to 5 years or 100,000 to 150,000 kilometers depending on the make and model. Customers should refer to the specific manufacturer documentation provided at the time of sale for exact terms.

3. Extended Warranty Options
Customers may purchase an extended warranty plan at the time of sale or within 30 days of delivery. We offer three tiers:

- Bronze Plan: Covers powertrain components for up to 12 months or 20,000 km. Cost: EUR 299.
- Silver Plan: Covers powertrain, electrical system, air conditioning, and steering components for up to 18 months or 30,000 km. Cost: EUR 549.
- Gold Plan: Comprehensive bumper-to-bumper coverage excluding wear-and-tear items (brake pads, tires, wiper blades) for up to 24 months or 40,000 km. Cost: EUR 899.

All extended warranty plans include 24/7 roadside assistance within Lithuania and the Baltic states.

4. Warranty Claim Procedure
To file a warranty claim, customers must:
a) Contact our service department at service@autogroupmotors.lt or call +370 5 123 4567.
b) Provide the vehicle identification number (VIN), date of purchase, and a description of the issue.
c) Schedule an inspection at our service center or an approved partner workshop.
d) If the claim is approved, repairs will be completed at no cost to the customer. A courtesy vehicle may be provided for repairs expected to take longer than 48 hours.

5. Exclusions
Warranty coverage does not apply to damage caused by accidents, misuse, unauthorized modifications, racing, neglected maintenance, or natural disasters. Wear-and-tear items such as brake pads, tires, filters, and windshield wipers are not covered under any warranty plan.

6. After-Sales Service
We offer scheduled maintenance packages at competitive rates. Customers who purchased a vehicle from us receive a 15% discount on labor for all service work performed at our facility during the first 12 months of ownership. We also provide complimentary multi-point inspections at 6-month intervals for the first year.

7. Customer Support
Our after-sales team is available Monday through Friday, 8:00 to 18:00, and Saturday 9:00 to 14:00. Customers can reach us by phone, email, or through the live chat on our website at www.autogroupmotors.lt. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 4 business hours.
