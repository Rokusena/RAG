HR CARs — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
Customer Information Document | Version 2.0 | Updated: January 2025
====================================================================

This FAQ document covers the most common questions asked by HR CARs customers.
It covers topics including warranty, financing, vehicle history, inspection reports,
the registration transfer process in Lithuania, and general purchasing information.

====================================================================
SECTION A: GENERAL PURCHASING QUESTIONS
====================================================================

Q1. What types of vehicles does HR CARs sell?
----------------------------------------------
HR CARs specialises in quality used passenger vehicles (lengvieji automobiliai) sourced from
Lithuania and Western Europe (primarily Germany, Belgium, Netherlands, and Sweden). Our stock
typically includes popular European and Japanese brands such as Volkswagen, Toyota, BMW, Audi,
Skoda, Renault, Mercedes-Benz, Opel, Hyundai, and Kia. We focus on vehicles from 2010 onwards
with verified service history. We do not currently sell electric vehicles as our primary stock,
though hybrid models may appear in inventory. Our price range is generally EUR 4,000 to EUR 25,000.

Q2. How do I know the asking prices are fair?
----------------------------------------------
All HR CARs vehicle prices are researched against current Lithuanian and broader European
market data before listing. We check autoplius.lt averages, autogidas.lt comparisons, and
European price guides (e.g., EurotaxGlass). Our pricing reflects the vehicle's age, mileage,
condition, specification, and any investment we have made in servicing or repairs prior to
listing. We encourage customers to do their own research — if you find a comparable vehicle
listed at a significantly different price, bring it to our attention and we will discuss it.
Our goal is transparent, fair pricing — not the cheapest price regardless of quality.

Q3. Can I negotiate the price?
----------------------------------------------
Yes, limited negotiation is possible. Our sales consultants can offer a small discount
(typically up to 3% of the listed price) at their own discretion. Larger discounts require
manager approval and are considered on a case-by-case basis, taking into account the vehicle's
time on the lot, condition, and current market demand. If you are purchasing a vehicle for cash
and do not require financing assistance, this may also be factored into pricing discussions.
We will always try to reach a fair agreement that works for both parties.

Q4. Do you accept trade-ins?
----------------------------------------------
Yes, HR CARs accepts trade-in vehicles. Bring your vehicle to our dealership for a visual
inspection and we will provide a trade-in valuation based on current market data. Please bring
the vehicle's technical passport (techninis pasas) and any available service records. We typically
look up values using autoplius.lt market comparisons and may use CarVertical to check the
vehicle's history. The trade-in value is deducted from the purchase price of the new vehicle.
Trade-in valuations are valid for 14 days. We reserve the right to adjust a valuation if the
vehicle's condition differs from what was described.

Q5. What payment methods do you accept?
----------------------------------------------
We accept the following payment methods:
  - Cash (EUR): Up to EUR 10,000 per transaction in compliance with Lithuanian AML legislation.
    Transactions above EUR 10,000 may not be completed in cash.
  - Bank transfer (pavedimas): Full or partial payment by bank transfer to the HR CARs
    Swedbank account. Vehicles are handed over only after payment is confirmed as received.
  - Leasing/financing: We work with partner leasing companies including Citadele Lizingas,
    Luminor Lizingas, and Swedbank Lizingas. See Section C for financing details.
  - We do NOT accept cheques or cryptocurrency.

SECTION B: WARRANTY AND RETURN POLICY
====================================================================

Q6. Does every car come with a warranty?
----------------------------------------------
Yes. Every vehicle sold by HR CARs comes with a standard 3-month mechanical warranty from the
date of purchase. This warranty covers mechanical failures in the engine, gearbox, and cooling
system that arise during normal use and are not attributable to accidents or misuse by the buyer.
The warranty is provided directly by HR CARs — it is not a manufacturer warranty. For vehicles
that still have an active manufacturer warranty (e.g., a 2021 Kia with remaining factory cover),
that manufacturer warranty continues alongside our 3-month HR CARs warranty.

Q7. What exactly does the 3-month warranty cover?
----------------------------------------------
The HR CARs 3-month warranty covers:
  - Engine internal mechanical failure (e.g., bearing failure, piston rings under normal use)
  - Gearbox mechanical failure (excluding wear from misuse or lack of servicing)
  - Cooling system failures (radiator, thermostat, water pump)
  - Electrical system faults not caused by external damage or modification

The warranty does NOT cover:
  - Consumable parts: brake pads, tyres, wiper blades, light bulbs, filters, clutch disc
  - Any fault that was disclosed on the Vehicle Condition Sheet at the time of sale
  - Damage resulting from accidents, flood, fire, or misuse
  - Faults arising from modifications made by the customer after purchase
  - Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents, upholstery wear)

Q8. How do I make a warranty claim?
----------------------------------------------
To make a warranty claim within the 3-month period:
  1. Contact HR CARs by phone (+370 600 00000) or email (hrcars@example.lt) to describe the issue.
  2. We will ask you to bring the vehicle to our partner service centre for an assessment.
  3. Our team will review the claim within 5 business days of receiving the vehicle.
  4. If the claim is valid, we will arrange the repair at no cost to you.
  5. If the fault is outside warranty scope, we will explain why and may recommend a repair
     service at a fair market rate.
Please do NOT have warranty repairs done at a third-party garage without first contacting HR CARs,
as unauthorised repairs will void the warranty claim.

Q9. What is your return policy?
----------------------------------------------
HR CARs offers a 14-day return policy on all vehicles sold. If you are unsatisfied with the
vehicle within 14 days of purchase, you may return it under the following conditions:
  - The vehicle must be returned in the same condition as it was sold (no new damage)
  - The additional mileage driven since purchase must not exceed 500 km
  - You must notify HR CARs in writing (email or letter) of your intent to return before the
    14-day period expires
  - A full refund of the purchase price will be issued within 5 business days of the return
    being accepted, via the original payment method
Note: The 14-day return policy applies to the sale price only. Any registration, insurance,
or government fees paid by the customer to REGITRA or insurance providers are not refundable
by HR CARs — these are third-party costs.

Q10. Can I extend the warranty beyond 3 months?
----------------------------------------------
Yes. At the time of sale, HR CARs can present you with options for extended warranty coverage
through third-party warranty providers. These products typically offer 6, 12, or 24 months of
extended mechanical coverage at an additional cost (usually EUR 150–500 depending on the vehicle
and coverage level). Extended warranty options must be arranged at the point of sale or within
30 days of purchase. Please ask your sales consultant for current extended warranty options.

SECTION C: FINANCING AND LEASING
====================================================================

Q11. Do you offer car financing / leasing?
----------------------------------------------
Yes, HR CARs partners with several Lithuanian leasing companies to offer customers flexible
financing options. Our partners include:
  - Citadele Lizingas
  - Luminor Lizingas
  - Swedbank Lizingas
A typical financing structure for a used vehicle at HR CARs is:
  - Down payment: 20–30% of the vehicle price
  - Loan/lease term: 12 to 60 months (1–5 years)
  - Annual interest rate: approximately 5–9% depending on your credit profile and the lender
  - Monthly instalment example: A EUR 10,000 vehicle with 25% down (EUR 2,500) over 48 months
    at 7% p.a. = approximately EUR 181/month
These are indicative figures — the leasing company provides final terms after credit assessment.

Q12. What documents do I need to apply for financing?
----------------------------------------------
To submit a financing application through HR CARs, you will typically need:
  - Lithuanian personal identity document (asmens tapatybės kortelė or pasas)
  - Personal identification number (asmens kodas)
  - Proof of income: recent payslips (last 3 months), employer contact, or bank statements
  - If self-employed: last year's tax declaration (GPM311 or equivalent)
HR CARs will submit the application to the leasing company on your behalf. You do not need
to visit the bank directly. Results are typically received within 1–2 business hours for
standard applications, or up to 1 business day for more complex cases.

Q13. Can I get financing if I have had credit problems in the past?
----------------------------------------------
Each leasing company assesses applications independently based on your current financial
situation, income, and credit history (checked via Creditinfo Lietuva). Past credit issues
do not automatically mean rejection, but they may affect the interest rate or down payment
required. We recommend being open with our team about your situation — we will identify the
most suitable lender for your profile. If standard leasing is not available, we can discuss
alternative options such as a higher down payment.

SECTION D: VEHICLE HISTORY AND INSPECTION
====================================================================

Q14. How do I know the vehicle's history is accurate?
----------------------------------------------
Every vehicle in HR CARs inventory has been checked using CarVertical (carvertical.com), a
comprehensive vehicle history database that aggregates data from multiple European sources
including national vehicle registers, insurance claim databases, EU total loss records, and
odometer verification databases. The CarVertical report shows:
  - Recorded mileage history across different countries and dates (to verify no odometer fraud)
  - Accident and damage records (severity and repair status)
  - Ownership history and country of first registration
  - Stolen vehicle check
  - Outstanding finance check (in some markets)
The full CarVertical report for any vehicle is available for you to review before purchase.
We will print it or display it digitally — just ask your sales consultant.

Q15. Can I have the car independently inspected before I buy it?
----------------------------------------------
Absolutely, and we strongly encourage it. HR CARs is transparent about our vehicles and
welcomes independent pre-purchase inspections. You may:
  a) Arrange for an independent mechanic to inspect the vehicle at our premises (subject to
     scheduling — please arrange in advance)
  b) We can arrange transportation of the vehicle to a nearby inspection centre of your
     choosing at our shared cost (fuel, short-distance transport)
  c) You may book a standard technical inspection at any REGITRA-certified station
We want you to buy with confidence. We will never rush you or discourage an inspection.

Q16. What is a techninė apžiūra and is it included with the car?
----------------------------------------------
Techninė apžiūra (TA) is Lithuania's mandatory vehicle roadworthiness inspection, similar to the
MOT in the UK or TÜV in Germany. All vehicles registered in Lithuania must pass periodic TA
inspections at REGITRA or authorised inspection centres. The inspection checks brakes, lights,
emissions, steering, suspension, and other safety-relevant systems.

At HR CARs, we ensure that every vehicle we sell has:
  - A valid TA with at least 3 months remaining, OR
  - A fresh TA arranged before sale if the existing one has less than 3 months remaining

The TA certificate is included with the vehicle documents. Ask your consultant to show you
the current TA validity date on the vehicle's technical documentation.

SECTION E: REGISTRATION AND LEGAL PROCESS
====================================================================

Q17. How do I re-register the car in my name after purchase?
----------------------------------------------
After purchasing a vehicle from HR CARs, you must register it in your name at a REGITRA
service point. Here is the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Purchase insurance. Before re-registration, you must purchase a valid compulsory
third-party liability insurance (TPVCA, commonly called "žalioji kortelė" — green card)
in your own name. You can buy this from any Lithuanian insurer (BTA, Ergo, UNIQA, Lietuvos
draudimas, etc.) online, by phone, or in person. Cost: approximately EUR 50–200/year depending
on the vehicle, your age, and driving record.

Step 2: Book a REGITRA appointment online at regitra.lt or call them. Vilnius has multiple
REGITRA offices. You can also use self-service terminals in some locations.

Step 3: Bring the following to REGITRA:
  - Your identity document (asmens tapatybės kortelė or pasas)
  - The signed pirkimo-pardavimo sutartis (purchase agreement)
  - The vehicle's technical passport (Transporto priemonės registracijos liudijimas)
  - Proof of valid TPVCA insurance in your name
  - Valid TA certificate (if applicable)
  - Payment for re-registration fee (approximately EUR 14–35 depending on vehicle)

Step 4: REGITRA will update the ownership record in the national vehicle register and issue
a new registration certificate. New registration plates may or may not be needed — you may
keep existing Lithuanian plates if you wish, or request new ones.

Step 5: The whole process at REGITRA typically takes 20–40 minutes.

HR CARs can assist you in preparing the documents and booking the REGITRA appointment.

Q18. Do I need to change the number plates after buying the car?
----------------------------------------------
In Lithuania, registration plates are tied to the vehicle, not the owner. When you buy a vehicle
from HR CARs, you can keep the existing Lithuanian plates — you do not need to change them.
The plates transfer with the vehicle to your name automatically upon re-registration at REGITRA.
However, if you prefer new plates (e.g., you want a personalised plate, or the current plates
are damaged), you may request new ones at REGITRA for an additional fee. If the vehicle was
previously registered abroad and has foreign plates, new Lithuanian plates must be issued during
the Lithuanian first-registration process.

Q19. What happens if there is outstanding finance on the car I want to buy?
----------------------------------------------
HR CARs thoroughly checks every vehicle using CarVertical and direct verification before
purchasing it for our inventory. We do not knowingly purchase vehicles with outstanding
finance (lien/pledge). If a vehicle in our inventory is found to have undisclosed outstanding
finance at any point before or after the sale, HR CARs will take full responsibility for
resolving the issue and will not pass this liability to the customer.
If you are buying from a private seller (not from HR CARs), we recommend always checking
for outstanding finance before purchase using the Lithuanian Credit Bureau (Creditinfo) or
REGITRA records to avoid inheriting another person's debt.

Q20. What documents will I receive when I buy a car from HR CARs?
----------------------------------------------
Upon completing your purchase from HR CARs, you will receive the following:
  1. Signed pirkimo-pardavimo sutartis (Vehicle Purchase-Sale Agreement) — your legal proof
     of purchase and ownership transfer
  2. Vehicle technical passport (Transporto priemonės registracijos liudijimas) — the official
     vehicle registration document
  3. Valid Techninė apžiūra (TA) certificate — roadworthiness inspection certificate
  4. CarVertical history report printout — for your records
  5. Vehicle Condition Sheet — documenting the vehicle's condition at time of sale
  6. All available service records and maintenance books
  7. All physical keys (we disclose how many key sets are available per vehicle)
  8. HR CARs Warranty Card — outlining your 3-month mechanical warranty terms
  9. HR CARs Return Policy statement — confirming your 14-day return rights
  10. Sales receipt / invoice

If any of the above documents are missing (e.g., second key set not available), this will be
disclosed before purchase and may be reflected in the price.
