EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
------------------
January 2025 was a solid opening month for HR CARs. Despite the traditionally slower post-New
Year period for vehicle sales in Lithuania, the dealership achieved 7 vehicle sales against a
target of 6. Vehicle acquisition activity was active with 5 new units purchased for inventory.
Revenue exceeded the EUR 100,000 mark for the month. After all operating expenses, the dealership
recorded a net profit of EUR 7,340, which is in line with our seasonal expectations for January.
Detailed figures are presented below.
