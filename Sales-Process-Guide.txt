HR CARs — SALES PROCESS GUIDE
Employee Reference Manual | Version 1.4 | Effective: January 2025
====================================================================

PURPOSE
--------
This guide outlines the complete step-by-step sales process that all HR CARs sales staff must
follow when handling a customer — from their very first contact through to vehicle handover and
post-sale follow-up. Following this process consistently ensures a high-quality customer experience,
legal compliance, and accurate record-keeping.

====================================================================
STAGE 1: FIRST CONTACT
====================================================================

1.1 WALK-IN CUSTOMERS
-----------------------
When a customer enters the showroom or walks onto the vehicle lot:
  - Greet them within 2 minutes: "Sveiki atvykę į HR CARs! Kuo galiu jums padėti?"
  - Do not swarm the customer immediately — give them 60 seconds to look around first if
    they appear to be browsing. Then approach calmly and professionally.
  - Introduce yourself by name: "Mano vardas [Vardas], aš esu HR CARs pardavimų konsultantas."
  - Ask open-ended questions to understand their needs:
    * "Kokio tipo automobilį jus domina?"
    * "Koks jūsų biudžetas?"
    * "Ar ieškote benzininio, dyzelinio, ar elektrinio automobilio?"
    * "Ar planuojate išsimokėtinai ar mokėsite iš karto?"

1.2 PHONE INQUIRIES
---------------------
  - Answer within 3 rings. Greeting: "HR CARs, laba diena, [vardas] klausau."
  - Take the customer's name and callback number immediately in case the call drops.
  - Listen carefully to what vehicle they are asking about.
  - If the vehicle is available, offer to book a viewing appointment.
  - Log the call in the CRM system: customer name, phone number, vehicle of interest, date/time.

1.3 ONLINE INQUIRIES (autoplius.lt, autogidas.lt, email, social media)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Respond within 1 business hour during working hours.
  - Include: vehicle availability confirmation, current listing price, and invitation to visit.
  - Template response example:
    "Sveiki, [Vardas]! Dėkojame už jūsų užklausą. [Automobilis] yra laisvai prieinamas apžiūrai.
     Norėdami suplanuoti apžiūrą, prašome susisiekti tel. +370 600 00000 arba atsakyti į šį laišką.
     Laukiame jūsų! — HR CARs komanda."
  - Enter all online leads into the CRM within 30 minutes of receiving them.

====================================================================
STAGE 2: VEHICLE PRESENTATION
====================================================================

2.1 Once you understand what the customer is looking for, escort them to the most suitable
    vehicle(s) in stock.

2.2 During the vehicle walk-around, cover the following points:
    a) Key features and spec level of the vehicle
    b) Year, mileage, and ownership history
    c) Any condition notes (be fully transparent — point out any cosmetic issues proactively)
    d) Carvertical / vehicle history report results
    e) Service history availability
    f) Current roadworthiness certificate (techninė apžiūra) validity date
    g) Remaining HR CARs warranty coverage (3 months from date of sale)

2.3 Never hide or downplay known defects. If a customer later discovers an undisclosed defect,
    this creates legal risk and damages the company's reputation.

2.4 If the customer is interested in multiple vehicles, present them side-by-side.
    Use the printed vehicle condition sheets (available in each vehicle's folder in the glove box).

====================================================================
STAGE 3: TEST DRIVE
====================================================================

3.1 Always offer a test drive to any serious buyer. "Ar norėtumėte išbandyti šį automobilį
    kelyje?"

3.2 PRE-TEST DRIVE REQUIREMENTS:
    a) Check that the customer has a valid driving licence. Ask to see it and record the
       licence number on the Test Drive Consent Form.
    b) Make a copy (photocopy or phone photo) of the driving licence for our records.
    c) Check that the vehicle is insured for test drives (HR CARs carries a fleet policy
       covering third-party liability for test drives with a licensed driver).
    d) The customer must sign the Test Drive Consent Form before any test drive begins.
       This form is available at the sales desk and covers: customer details, vehicle details,
       odometer at start, agreed route, and liability conditions.

3.3 TEST DRIVE ROUTE:
    - The agreed test drive route covers approximately 5–10 km.
    - The route includes: urban speed (up to 50 km/h), a short stretch of 70–90 km/h road,
      and a stop/start section to test braking and clutch/gearbox.
    - An HR CARs employee must be present in the vehicle at all times during the test drive
      (seated in the front passenger seat for saloon/hatchback, rear left for SUV if preferred).

3.4 POST-TEST DRIVE:
    - Record the odometer at return on the Test Drive Consent Form.
    - Ask the customer for their impressions: "Kaip patiko? Ar turėjote klausimų apie automobilį?"
    - Use this moment to address any concerns and move toward closing.

====================================================================
STAGE 4: NEGOTIATION AND PRICING
====================================================================

4.1 If the customer wishes to negotiate on price:
    - Sales staff may discount up to 3% of the listing price without manager approval.
    - Discounts beyond 3% must be approved in writing (WhatsApp or email) by the manager.
    - Do not show desperation or anxiety during negotiation. Be calm and confident.
    - Remind the customer of the value: inspection, warranty, clean history report, etc.

4.2 If the customer has a trade-in vehicle:
    - Inspect the trade-in vehicle briefly (exterior, interior, start the engine, check lights).
    - Note the mileage, VIN, and registration details.
    - Look up the vehicle's current market value on autoplius.lt.
    - Do NOT give a final trade-in value on the spot. Say: "Aš turiu pasikonsultuoti su
      vadovybe — atsakysiu jums per 30 minučių."
    - Present the trade-in valuation to management for approval before communicating to the customer.

4.3 FINANCING OPTIONS:
    - HR CARs works with partner leasing companies (e.g., Citadele Lizingas, Luminor Lizingas,
      Swedbank Lizingas).
    - If a customer is interested in financing, collect: full name, personal ID number (asmens
      kodas), monthly income estimate, and employer details.
    - Submit the financing inquiry to the relevant partner using their broker portal.
    - Typical lease terms: 20–30% down payment, 12–60 month term, interest rate 5–9% p.a.
    - Inform the customer that final approval is subject to the leasing company's credit assessment.
    - Do not guarantee approval — this is strictly the leasing company's decision.

====================================================================
STAGE 5: SALE AGREEMENT — PIRKIMO-PARDAVIMO SUTARTIS
====================================================================

5.1 Once the customer agrees to purchase, a formal Vehicle Purchase-Sale Agreement
    (pirkimo-pardavimo sutartis) must be prepared.

5.2 The agreement must include:
    a) Full legal name and personal ID (asmens kodas) of the buyer
    b) Full legal name and company details of HR CARs as the seller
    c) Vehicle details: make, model, VIN (kėbulo numeris), year, engine number, colour,
       registration plate (if already registered in Lithuania)
    d) Agreed sale price in EUR (both numerals and written in words)
    e) Odometer reading at time of sale (confirmed by both parties)
    f) List of any known defects or condition notes (reference to the Vehicle Condition Sheet)
    g) Payment method (cash / bank transfer / financing)
    h) Warranty terms: 3-month HR CARs mechanical warranty conditions
    i) Return policy terms: 14-day return policy conditions
    j) Date and place of signing
    k) Signatures of both buyer and seller (or authorised HR CARs representative)

5.3 The agreement must be prepared in two original copies — one for the customer, one for
    HR CARs records. Both copies must be signed before payment is processed.

5.4 A template pirkimo-pardavimo sutartis is stored in the shared drive under:
    /Templates/Sales/PPS_Template_2025.docx
    Fill in all fields carefully. Never leave fields blank — write N/A if not applicable.

====================================================================
STAGE 6: PAYMENT HANDLING
====================================================================

6.1 CASH PAYMENTS:
    - Accept only up to EUR 10,000 in cash per transaction, in compliance with Lithuanian
      anti-money laundering (AML) regulations (Pinigų plovimo ir teroristų finansavimo
      prevencijos įstatymas).
    - Cash above EUR 10,000 must be paid by bank transfer. Inform the customer of this policy.
    - Count cash in front of the customer using the cash counting machine located at the desk.
    - Issue a cash receipt (kasos čekis) immediately.
    - Deposit all cash the same business day into the company bank account at Swedbank.

6.2 BANK TRANSFER:
    - Provide the customer with the company's bank account details:
      Bank: Swedbank AB
      Account holder: HR CARs UAB
      IBAN: LT00 7300 0000 0000 0000 (example — use actual account)
      Payment reference: [Vehicle Registration / VIN]
    - The vehicle must not be handed over until the full payment is confirmed as received
      in the company account. This may take 1–2 business days for inter-bank transfers.

6.3 FINANCING / LEASING:
    - Once the leasing company confirms approval and disburses funds to HR CARs, issue
      the standard invoice to the leasing company (not the customer).
    - Handover can proceed once funds are confirmed received.

====================================================================
STAGE 7: VEHICLE REGISTRATION TRANSFER (PERREGISTRAVIMAS)
====================================================================

7.1 Once payment is complete and the PPS is signed, the vehicle must be re-registered to
    the new owner. In Lithuania, this is done at REGITRA (Valstybės įmonė "Regitra").

7.2 Required documents for re-registration:
    a) Signed pirkimo-pardavimo sutartis (original)
    b) Vehicle's technical passport (techninis pasas / Transporto priemonės registracijos liudijimas)
    c) Buyer's passport or ID card
    d) Proof of valid third-party liability insurance (TPVCA / "žalioji kortelė") in the buyer's name
       — the buyer must purchase insurance before re-registration
    e) Valid techninė apžiūra (roadworthiness certificate)

7.3 HR CARs can assist the customer in booking a REGITRA appointment online at regitra.lt.
    We recommend same-day or next-day appointments where possible.

7.4 The vehicle may not be driven on public roads under the seller's registration plates after
    the PPS is signed. New owner must register before driving the vehicle away, OR use a
    transit permit (tranzitiniai numeriai) if driving to REGITRA.

7.5 HR CARs will retain a copy of the signed PPS and record the sale in the company
    vehicle register. The vehicle's entry in the inventory system must be updated to SOLD
    immediately after handover.

====================================================================
STAGE 8: VEHICLE HANDOVER
====================================================================

8.1 Before handing over the keys:
    - Confirm payment is received in full
    - Confirm PPS is signed by both parties
    - Confirm the customer has valid insurance in their name
    - Confirm the vehicle has been cleaned (interior vacuum and exterior wash)
    - Prepare a Vehicle Handover Checklist: keys (how many sets?), service books, spare tyre,
      jack, warning triangle, locking wheel nut key if applicable

8.2 Walk through the vehicle with the customer one final time:
    - Demonstrate key functions (infotainment, lights, wipers, etc.)
    - Remind them of the 3-month warranty conditions and 14-day return policy
    - Provide them with HR CARs contact details for after-sales support

8.3 Ask the customer to sign the Vehicle Handover Receipt confirming they have received
    the vehicle and all listed items in the agreed condition.

8.4 Take a short handover photo with the customer (if they consent) for social media/records.

====================================================================
STAGE 9: POST-SALE FOLLOW-UP
====================================================================

9.1 Call or message the customer 7 days after handover:
    "Sveiki [Vardas], čia [jūsų vardas] iš HR CARs. Norėjome pasiteirauti, kaip sekasi su
    nauju automobiliu? Ar viskas gerai?"

9.2 Log the follow-up call result in the CRM system.

9.3 If the customer has any concerns:
    - Minor issues — advise on the warranty claim process
    - Complaints — escalate to manager immediately

9.4 After 30 days, send a polite SMS or email asking for a Google Maps / autoplius.lt review.
    Positive reviews are vital for the dealership's online reputation.

9.5 Add the customer to the HR CARs newsletter/loyalty database (with their consent) for
    future promotions, trade-in opportunities, and new stock announcements.
